OUR VISION

The core theme of the exhibition is "Relating Through Memory," emphasizing how personal experiences become universal when shared without context. The anonymity of the submissions will allow the curators to present these stories in a way that anyone can project their emotions, allowing the exhibition to take on new significance for each visitor. By presenting these memories through artistic interpretations, we encourage visitors to see fragments of their own experiences in others. The theme holds significance as it explores human connectivity, empathy, and the nature of storytelling.
